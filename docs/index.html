How scoring works

Calibrated from 41 Motive venture investments on motivepartners.com. Hard filters first; then portfolio-weighted ranking.

1 · Strict mandate gates

Three binary filters — all must pass: Pre-Seed – Series A, US or Europe HQ, and core fintech sector.

2 · Portfolio-calibrated sector fit (19%)

Sector weights mirror Motive's venture portfolio: Wealth 34%, Banking/Payments 32%, AI/Data 15%.

3 · Thesis similarity (17%)

TF-IDF cosine similarity vs Motive thesis statements and 37 venture portfolio references.

4 · Geography & company age (6% each)

Hub-city bonus for NYC, Berlin, London, SF. Company age scored against stage-typical windows.

5 · Deal economics & moat (6% each)

Check size fit ($1–10M), capital efficiency, infrastructure moat, and vertical AI fit.

6 · Traction, founders, stage (13% / 11% / 4%)

ARR/GMV/growth parsing, tier-1 fintech founder signals, and stage fit within mandate.

7 · CSV upload

Accepts common header aliases. UTF-8 BOM safe. Column-mapping UI for non-standard headers.

8 · Output tiers

≥76 Priority Review · 60–75 Standard Review · <60 Low Priority.
