How scoring works

Scoring is calibrated against 38 active Motive venture investments on motivepartners.com/portfolio (Venture filter; 41 total entries). Realized/exited positions are excluded from the similarity corpus. Hard filters first; then portfolio-weighted ranking.

1 · Strict mandate gates

Two hard mandate gates — both must pass: Pre-Seed – Series A and US or Europe HQ. Adjacent sectors (HR Tech, PropTech, etc.) stay in the shortlist but score lower.

2 · Portfolio sector fit

Sector weights mirror Motive's 38-company venture corpus: Wealth 34%, Banking/Payments 32%, AI/Data 16%. Source: Motive venture portfolio.

3 · Thesis similarity

TF-IDF cosine similarity vs Motive thesis statements and 38 venture portfolio references. Raw cosine scores (typically 0.02–0.18) are rescaled to 0–100 for display (25 + cosine × 400).

4 · Deal economics

Check size fit ($1–10M per Motive Partners), capital efficiency, infrastructure moat, and vertical AI fit.

5 · Geography & age

Hub-city bonus (NYC, Berlin, London, SF). Company age scored against stage-typical windows.

6 · Output tiers

≥76 Priority Review · 60–75 Standard Review · <60 Low Priority. Every result includes reasons.
