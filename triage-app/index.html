How scoring works

Calibrated from 41 Motive venture investments on motivepartners.com. Hard filters first; then portfolio-weighted ranking.

1 · Strict mandate gates

Three binary filters — all must pass: Pre-Seed – Series A, US or Europe HQ, and core fintech sector (non-fintech such as HR, logistics, or pure healthcare IT are excluded with reasons).

2 · Portfolio-calibrated sector fit (24%)

Sector weights mirror Motive's venture portfolio: Wealth 34%, Banking/Payments 32%, AI/Data 15%, Capital Markets 10%, Insurance 7%, Business Services 2%.

3 · Thesis similarity (22%)

TF-IDF cosine similarity vs Motive thesis statements and 37 venture portfolio references — closest comp surfaced (e.g. Navro, Synthera AI).

4 · Geography & company age (8% each)

Hub-city bonus for NYC, Berlin, London, SF (~44% US / 56% Europe portfolio mix). Company age scored against stage-typical windows (e.g. Seed: 1–4 yrs).

5 · Traction, founders, stage (18% / 14% / 6%)

ARR/GMV/growth parsing, tier-1 fintech founder signals, and stage fit within mandate — each with human-readable reasons.

6 · Output tiers

≥76 Priority Review · 60–75 Standard Review · <60 Low Priority. Every result includes filter reasons or score rationale.
